About FileTrans

FileTrans supports a wide range of file conversions to meet your needs.

Supported Conversion Types

  • Fonts: TTF, OTF, WOFF, WOFF2
  • Images: PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, BMP, TIFF, WEBP
  • Documents: PDF to PNG/JPG/JPEG/DOCX/TXT, DOCX to PDF/TXT, TXT to PDF/DOCX/MD, ODT to DOCX/TXT, MD to PDF/TXT
  • Audio: MP3, WAV, OGG, FLAC, AAC, M4A
  • Video: MP4, AVI, MKV, MOV, WMV, FLV, WEBM
  • Archives: ZIP, TAR, GZ, 7Z
  • Data Files: CSV, XLS, XLSX to CSV/XLS/XLSX/JSON/TXT, JSON to CSV/TXT, XML to JSON/TXT
